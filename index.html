Lightsaber demo

Behold my moves in my, uh, bathrobe ... (it's the closest thing I had to a Jedi robe!)

Anyway, this video showcases a custom saber font I created using LBPitchBlack's "Pitch Black Evolution" SmoothSwing sound font coupled with Sabertrio's default Kylo Ren blade profile which I’ve tuned to be slightly more explosive on ignition and have an amber hue. The featured “Dark Edition Neo Reaver” lightsaber was purchased from Sabertrio and has consistently performed through all my saber font testing while maintaining fit and finish. Moreover, the 'brain' of this Reaver lightsaber is the CF-X microcontroller designed by Erv Plecter—this brain is what enables the stunning realism you see in the video, so thanks to Erv’s brilliance!

Additionally, my own lightsaber prototype has been fully fleshed out and the construction complete after a year’s work! It utilizes the same CF-X microcontroller and improves slightly upon the featured Reaver lightsaber’s experience because of its high bass speaker implementation and brighter LEDs within the blade! Some of the internal components I conceptualized and designed can be explored on my website at sunsabers.com. Please note that Sunsabers’ components are not for sale; it's just a web-gallery where my efforts are displayed so I can share with others the grueling (and fun!) year of manufacturing and assembling those parts into my elegant weapon.

Thanks for watching and may the Force be with you!
